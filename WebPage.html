Travel Tips & Local Guides

🍲 Must-Try Goan Food

  • Fish Curry Rice
  • Bebinca (layered dessert)
  • Goan Sausages
  • Feni (local spirit)

✅ Do’s in Goa

  • Respect local culture & churches
  • Try local seafood & markets
  • Use sunscreen & stay hydrated
  • Negotiate prices at flea markets

🚫 Don’ts in Goa

  • Don’t litter on beaches
  • Don’t take photos where prohibited
  • Don’t disturb wildlife or nature spots
  • Don’t drive under influence

🛍️ Local Markets to Explore

  • Anjuna Flea Market: Clothes, handicrafts, food
  • Mapusa Market: Fresh produce & spices
  • Arpora Night Market: Music, food, stalls
  • Panjim Market: Authentic Goan items

🚗 Transport Tips

  • Rent a scooter or bike for flexibility
  • Carry your driving license at all times
  • Use app-based taxis or prepaid cabs
  • Public buses are cheap but not frequent

💸 Currency & Payments

  • Carry some cash for local vendors
  • ATMs are widely available
  • UPI & cards accepted in most places
  • Avoid exchanging money at airports

🌐 Network & Connectivity

  • Get a local SIM for better coverage
  • Wi-Fi is available in cafes & hotels
  • Download offline maps for rural areas
  • Mobile data is fast & affordable

📞 Emergency Contacts

  • Tourist Helpline: 1363
  • Police: 100
  • Ambulance: 108
  • Nearest hospital or clinic info
